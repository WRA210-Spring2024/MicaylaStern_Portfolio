About Me!

Finding my Calling:

Prior to knowing what XA/UX was, I thought I was going to be a big shot Architect. I applied to a few schools out of state with my eyes glued on Georgia Tech. I quickly realized my heart was telling me to stay close to home so I applied to MSU.


I began my time at MSU studying computer science, however, it wasn't for me. I wanted to have a career that I knew could help individuals in the future. I had discovered XA and fell in love.
